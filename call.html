🏆 Objection: "I already have a system"

Objection
// Most common objection from agents "That's great — which one are you using? The reason I ask: most CRMs don't actually automate the response. They just log it. Our system goes from inquiry to text in under 60 seconds — no manual step in between. What's your current response time? Average is 12 hours. We get it down to 60 seconds. Mind if I show you the difference? 90 seconds."
🛑 "It's too expensive" / "I don't have the budget"
Respond with ROI framing:
"Totally fair. Let me put it in perspective — {297/month} breaks down to about {10/day}. If we generate just one extra customer per month, you've already made your money back 5x. Average ROI for our clients is 5-10x. Would you rather wait until you're losing more ground to competitors?" 💡 For AAA: Compare to cost of a single Zillow lead ($50-150). Our system is cheaper than 3 Zillow leads and generates unlimited.
🛑 "I need to think about it" / "Let me get back to you"
Soft close with timeline:
"I completely understand — it's a decision. What specifically would help you make the call? If it helps, we can start with a 30-day trial. If you're not seeing results, you pay nothing. No risk to you. When would be a good time to follow up? Thursday or Friday?"
🛑 "I'm not interested" (cold call)
Respectful exit with value:
"No problem at all — I respect your time. Before I go, one quick tip that'll cost you nothing: [LR] Start responding to every Google review within 24 hours. That alone will improve your local ranking. [AAA] Set up a text autoresponder for new leads — even a simple one. 60-second response is the difference. I'll leave you my info. If things change, I'm one call away."
🛑 "We already have someone managing our GBP/leads"
Differentiate or offer to improve:
"That's good — who are you using? I'm not here to replace them, but I can tell you within 2 minutes if there are gaps they're missing. Most agencies focus on one thing — we do a full audit across all dimensions. No charge. Want me to take a quick look?"
🛑 "Can you do it cheaper?"
Hold pricing, offer value:
"Our price is $297/mo for a reason — we do what works. I could cut corners and charge $99, but you'd get $99 results. The first month is a trial. If it's not worth every penny, you don't pay. What's the risk?" 💡 Never discount. The trial IS the risk reversal.
🛑 "I don't have time for this"
Flip it — WE save time:
"That's exactly why you need us. Our whole service is built on saving you time. [LR] We handle your Google profile so you don't have to. 10 minutes to set up, then it runs itself. [AAA] Our system follows up with leads so you don't have to. Set it once, runs on autopilot. We don't add to your plate. We take things off."
🛑 "My business is doing fine without it"
Acknowledge + future-proof:
"Glad to hear business is good! That's the best time to optimize — when you're not desperate. Your competitors are optimizing right now. In 6 months, they'll be ranking above you. We're not asking you to fix something broken — we're asking you to get ahead before your competition does. 30-day trial. You'll see the difference in 2 weeks."
